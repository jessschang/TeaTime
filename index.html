How to make tea

Step 1: Boil hot water

Boil some water and set aside

Step 2: Choose a tea

Choose a tea of your choice. My favorite is green tea with lemongrass.

Step 3: Steep your tea

Choose a mug of your choice, place the tea bag in it and pour the hot water in. Then let it steep for 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Enjoy
